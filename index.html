Watson and the shark

The painting depicts a dramatic scene in which a young man named Brook Watson is attacked by a shark while swimming in the Caribbean Sea. The event is based on a real incident that occurred in 1749 when Watson, who was a 14-year-old cabin boy at the time, was bitten by a shark while swimming in Havana Harbor.
